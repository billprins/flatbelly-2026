Flatbelly participants & spouses — you are cordially invited to the Saturday Night Couples Party, following the conclusion of the Flatbelly shootout. Join us for a night of denim, diamonds, drinks, dinner, and live music. Break out your best denim, add a little sparkle, and get ready for a great night celebrating Flatbelly.

Why You Stay Saturday Night
Break Out the Denim, Bring the Sparkle
The theme is Denim & Diamonds — your best denim, a little shine, and boots strictly encouraged. Come dressed for the occasion.
Toast the Pink Jacket Winners
The shootout wraps, the party starts, and the new champions celebrate in pink. Be in the room — not hearing about it Sunday morning.
MVP Takes the Stage
Six pieces, two lead vocalists, and choreography to match — Motown, funk, and Top-40 from 6 to 11. The dance floor won't fill itself, but MVP will make sure it does.
A Night for Two
Participants and spouses are cordially invited. Three days of golf deserve a proper finish — share it with your favorite plus-one.
Live Entertainment
MVP
