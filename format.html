Format
2-Man Team
Match Play
Five 9-hole matches — one against every other team in your flight · Full nine holes every match
Scoring
10 Points Per Match
1 point per hole won, ½ for a tied hole · 1 point for winning the match, ½ each if tied
Handicaps
80% of Full Handicap

Flights
11 Flights of Six
Set by combined team handicap index · Ties broken by the low player's tournament index, then current index

14 Teams, One Survivor
Eleven flight winners + three wildcards · Team alternate shot, sudden death · Saturday ~3:00 PM

USGA Handicap Required
