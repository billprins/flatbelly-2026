Information Important to Guests

Welcome to Hillwood! A few guidelines to make your visit smooth — your host and our staff will handle the rest.

Bag Drop
Follow the signs to the bag drop — staff will handle your clubs and shoes and escort you to the Golf Shop and your guest locker.
Locker Room
A guest locker is provided for your daily use. Please change your shoes in the locker room; attendants are ready to help.
Course Care
Return to the path before the cedar posts in the fairways; cart path only on all par 3s. Fill divots with sand, rake bunkers, repair pitch marks.
Pace of Play
First groups should play in 4 hours or less; stay in contact with the group ahead. Ready golf is encouraged.
Cell Phones
Phone calls are strongly discouraged anywhere on property. Silent use — texting and email — is permissible.
Attire
Collared or mock-neck golf shirts (tucked in), golf slacks or Bermuda-length shorts. No tee shirts, denim, cargo or gym shorts. Hats forward.
Smoking
Prohibited inside the clubhouse. Permitted in designated outdoor areas, and in the Men's Grill after 5:30 PM.
Tipping
Discretionary tipping is permitted for golf staff and locker room attendants.
Club Storage
Clubs are stored for the tournament — outside services loads them on your cart each morning, provides a guest bag tag, and cleans them after every round.
On-Course Food & Beverage
Food action stations on the front 9, back 9, and near the clubhouse throughout Friday and Saturday matches; beverages at the rest stations on holes 5 and 13.
