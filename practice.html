Practice Round Shootout
8 teams · alternate shot on 10, 11 & 9

Thursday is no throwaway warm-up — it’s an 18-hole, 2-man best ball tournament off the full shotgun, played at tournament handicaps. Finish top 6 net, low gross, or catch the Secret Wild Card and you’re in the evening shootout for the Championship Belt. And with the live Calcutta at 7:00, it’s the only intel anyone gets before the bidding starts.

Official starting holes are being finalized by the golf shop — check back here, they’ll be posted the moment the pairings sheet is released. Starting hole & cart assignments will also be in your registration packet.
