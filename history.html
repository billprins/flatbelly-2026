Hillwood Country Club · Nashville, Tennessee
The Flatbelly Tradition
Every club has a tournament where the trash talk starts weeks early and the results get argued about for months after. At Hillwood, that's the Flatbelly. Rick Tice & Steve Grossman took the first title in 1986, Bill Thomas won three, two teams went back-to-back, and after a decade-long hiatus the tournament roared back in 2016 — bigger every year since. In 2026, the 29th playing, three past championship teams are back in the field intact, and the defending champion is here to keep his jacket.

The Names on the Board
The Pink Jacket
Win the Flatbelly and you join every champion before you — permanently, and conspicuously. Evan Malone & Parker Hanks wore the pink home in 2025. In 2026, two more names go on the board — and Malone is back in the field to defend.
