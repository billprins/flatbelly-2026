Entry & Pools

The betting starts Thursday. After the practice round shootout, the field gathers in the Men's Grill for the live Calcutta — every team goes on the block, and the practice round is the only intel you get. It's a cash only event: bring cash.

  • Live Calcutta 7:00–9:00 PM Thursday in the Men's Grill · All teams auctioned
  • If another person buys your team, you have the right to purchase ½ the team from them — deal directly with the purchaser, settled before Friday tee-off
  • Player's Pool: mandatory $400 per team at registration, cash only — bring $200 per player
  • Tournament entry: $1,500 per team ($500 deposit was billed at the lottery draw)
