A Word from the Committee

Welcome to The Flatbelly — Hillwood Country Club's fiercest weekend of the late summer. Two days of golf on the best greens in Nashville, a Friday night that runs long, a Saturday shootout that decides everything, and an awards party worth dressing up for. Bring your partner, bring your game, and leave the excuses at home.

The Course — Hillwood Country Club

Originally designed by Dick Wilson and opened in 1957, Hillwood's par 72 stretches over 7,000 yards. Renovated by Bruce Hepner of Renaissance Golf in 2003 and 2011 — home to the best greens in Nashville.
