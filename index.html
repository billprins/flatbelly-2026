A Word from the Committee

Welcome to The Flatbelly — three days of two-man team match play on the best greens in Nashville. Five 9-hole matches against your flight decide eleven flight winners, three wildcards sneak in, and Saturday's alternate-shot shootout settles it in front of everyone. The last team standing wears pink. Bring your partner, bring your game, and leave the excuses at home.

Registration — Thursday 10:00–12:00 by the First Tee
Pick up your player packet and Holderness & Bourne gift package, take your team photo, and enter the mandatory $400 team Player's Pool — bring $200 cash per player (cash only). Can't make Thursday? Register in the golf shop Friday morning before play. All players must have an established USGA handicap; guests without one play to scratch. Questions? Call the golf shop at 615-352-5600 or email Chris Zumpano at chris.zumpano@hillwoodcc.org.
