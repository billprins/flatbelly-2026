A Word from the Committee

Welcome to The Flatbelly — three days of team best ball on the best greens in Nashville. Five 9-hole matches decide eleven flight winners, one wildcard sneaks in, and a Saturday shootout settles it in front of everyone. The last team standing wears pink. Bring your partner, bring your game, and leave the excuses at home.

Flatbelly participants & spouses — you are cordially invited to the Saturday Night Couples Party, following the conclusion of the Flatbelly shootout. Why You Stay Saturday Night
Break Out the Denim, Bring the Sparkle
The theme is Denim & Diamonds — your best denim, a little shine, and boots strictly encouraged. Come dressed for the occasion.
Toast the Pink Jacket Winners
The shootout wraps, the party starts, and the new champions celebrate in pink. Be in the room — not hearing about it Sunday morning.
Drinks, Dinner & Live Music
All three, all night — from 6 to 11. The club is saving its best for Saturday, and the dance floor won't fill itself.
A Night for Two
Participants and spouses are cordially invited. Three days of golf deserve a proper finish — share it with your favorite plus-one.
The Course — Hillwood Country Club

A Dick Wilson design playing 7,059 yards from the back tees, refined through the club's ongoing partnership with Bruce Hepner of Renaissance Golf Design — home to the best greens in Nashville.

Hillwood has hosted the Tennessee State Open (1963, '66, '71), the Tennessee State Amateur (1975, '82, 2004, 2020), the Tennessee Mid-Am (1998), the Tennessee Senior Amateur (1986, 2001, 2013) and the 2015 U.S. Women's Senior Amateur.

Information Important to Guests

Welcome to Hillwood! A few guidelines to make your visit smooth — your host and our staff will handle the rest.

Bag Drop
Follow the signs to the bag drop — staff will handle your clubs and shoes and escort you to the Golf Shop and your guest locker.
Locker Room
A guest locker is provided for your daily use. Please change your shoes in the locker room; attendants are ready to help.
Course Care
Return to the path before the cedar posts in the fairways; cart path only on all par 3s. Fill divots with sand, rake bunkers, repair pitch marks.
Pace of Play
First groups should play in 4 hours or less; stay in contact with the group ahead. Ready golf is encouraged.
Cell Phones
Phone calls are strongly discouraged anywhere on property. Silent use — texting and email — is permissible.
Attire
Collared or mock-neck golf shirts (tucked in), golf slacks or Bermuda-length shorts. No tee shirts, denim, cargo or gym shorts. Hats forward.
Smoking
Prohibited inside the clubhouse. Permitted in designated outdoor areas, and in the Men's Grill after 5:30 PM.
Tipping
Discretionary tipping is permitted for golf staff and locker room attendants.
